ABOUT FRUITS

Berries: Fleshy fruits with seeds embedded inside. Botanically, Bananas and Grapes are berries, but strawberries are not. Drupes (Stone Fruits): Fleshy fruits with a hard inner "stone" or pit containing the seed (e.g., Cherries, Plums, and Mangoes). Pomes: Fruits with a central core containing seeds surrounded by fleshy tissue (e.g., Apples and Pears). Dry Fruits: Fruits that are hard and dry at maturity, including nuts, legumes (beans/peas), and cereal grains (wheat/rice). Wikipedia
